Contact

Directly contact us at staff _at_ somervillebikekitchen (dot) org.

Subscribe to our "discuss" mailing list to keep up to date and join the discussion:

  1. send a blank email to discuss _plus sign_ subscribe (at) somervillebikekitchen *dot* org
  2. wait for confirmation email and follow instructions to join

Chat with us in our irc channel (#sbk on irc.freenode.net)

Find us at 339R Summer Street, Somerville, MA
