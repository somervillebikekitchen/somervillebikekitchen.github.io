--- layout: event title: Flat Fixing Workshop datetime: 2016-12-08 (Thu) 18:00 location: Sprout, 339R Summer St, Somerville (home to SBK) description: learn how to fix flats on the road ---

Punctures are inevitable, and they are inconvenient no matter the distance from your destination. Maybe the thought of getting a flat has discouraged you from travelling long distances by bicycle. Fortunately, a patch kit and some know-how is really all you need to keep pedaling.

During Thursday 12/8 open shop hours, Troy will be around to answer your questions about flats and show you how to patch inner tubes.
