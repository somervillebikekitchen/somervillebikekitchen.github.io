First Open Shop

2015-05-12 18:00-21:00

SBK opens its doors for the first time!

---

Hey Local Bicyclists!

Announcing the start of Somerville Bike Kitchen's (SBK) Open Shop nights every Tuesday evening from 6pm to 9pm. SBK is a community organized bike repair space that provides tools and stands to help people to fix their own bikes. The space is located at 339R Summer St. just outside of Davis Sq. We have a suggested $10 per hour use fee.

Our first open shop is this Tuesday (5/12), so stop by to fix your bike our just check us out! You can also find more information on the web at omervillebikekitchen.org.
