--- layout: event title: Bicycle Brazing Workshop 1/2 datetime: 2016-03-01 (Tue) 18:00-21:00 location: Sprout, 339R Summer St, Somerville (home to SBK) description: bicycle brazing discussion and brazing demonstration ---

Prospective framebuilders or modders! Learn how to braze accessories onto steel bicycle frames!

During this session we will have a open-ended discussion about general brazing and bicycle brazing. Topics covered: tools, materials, techniques. We will briefly demonstrate brazing with some scrap, and show off some examples of finished braze jobs we've done before.

For those interested, we will help you start positioning and mitering your braze-on accessories for installation on your steel bicycle frame. Come prepared with your braze-ons or have them delivered before session 2 of this workshop! We have a small collection of extra braze-on accessories (seat bolt bosses, canti bosses, rack eyelets, etc.) which you are free to use, but the selection is limited.

During the next session you will perform the actual brazing.

Suggested contribution: $10
