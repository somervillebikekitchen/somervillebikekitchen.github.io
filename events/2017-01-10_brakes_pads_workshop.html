--- layout: event title: "Brakes & Brake Pads Workshop" datetime: 2017-01-10 (Tue) 18:30 location: Sprout, 339R Summer St, Somerville (home to SBK) description: learn how to adjust brakes ---

An introduction to how to tune up your brakes and replace your brake pads. We will walk you through how to assess your brakes for damage/wear, clean them, and align them. This workshop will focus on rim brakes, but disc brakes may be discussed if there is interest. If you wish to replace your brake pads/cartridges please bring them with you.
