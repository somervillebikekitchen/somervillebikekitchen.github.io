Staff

Shift matrix:

To request to be scheduled for a certain day, send mail to info@.

Staff requirements:

SBK requires that all staff:
  • host open shop once a month
  • pay staff dues ($18/mo payable in 3 month increments)

How to host open shop:

  1. arrive on time to unlock.
  2. put out the sign (usually a whiteboard propped up on a chair).
  3. at the end of open shop:
    • put away the tools.
    • count the money in the "contributions" box and transfer it to the money jar.
    • record the amount transferred in the notepad on the wall.
  4. lock up.

If you can't make your scheduled shift, please notify info@ soon so we can find somebody to cover.

How to contact other staff:

Simply send mail to info@. All staff will receive mail sent to info@.
