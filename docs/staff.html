Staff

Shift matrix:

To request to be scheduled for a certain day, send mail to info _at_ somervillebikekitchen (dot) org.

Staff requirements:

  • host open shop once a month
  • pay staff dues ($18/mo)

How to host open shop:

  1. arrive on time to unlock.
  2. put out the sign (usually a whiteboard propped up on a chair).
  3. at the end of open shop, put away the tools.
  4. lock up.
